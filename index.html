Matariki at the Christchurch Botanic Gardens


Join us at the botanic gardens from the 10th to the 12th of July for some Matariki fun!

From 4pm til 10pm there will be a series of performances, live traditional music, and food trucks/stalls located all around the gardens.

With 4 main stages set around the garden, you’ll never be far from the action. Between the stages, there will be plenty of food trucks and light installations to feast both your eyes and your puku.
